Barcelona


Barcelona is famous for its outstanding football team, stunning architecture,lively night life,sandy beaches and world class cuisine.not to mention vibrant cultural heritage and colourful neighbourhood festivals that attract visitors from all around the world.The city of Barcelona dates back to the times of roman empire,two thousand years ago when it was named Barcio.Remains of the original Roman-era buildings can still be seen today,although most of them have been built over or incorporated into medieval buildings which replaced them due to its strategic location,the city quickly grew up in importance by the middle ages,Barcelona was home to the counts who ruled the city and the surrounding area and was the facto capital of the crown of argon.
